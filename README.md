### About

I'm an electrical engineering student working at the intersection of hardware and software. My
coursework is grounded in circuits, signals, and control systems, and most of my building happens
where that meets code — embedded firmware, sensor instrumentation, and the tooling that supports
them. I learn by taking projects end to end, from the bench to the repository, and I care about
understanding systems deeply rather than just getting them to run.

### Experience & research

- **Undergraduate research — biosensor instrumentation.** Hardware and signal work in a
  neonatal-care research lab. Co-author on the grant funding the position and a contributor to a
  forthcoming publication.
- **Engineering co-op — Salas O'Brien.** Controls and automation work involving PLCs and
  industrial control systems.
